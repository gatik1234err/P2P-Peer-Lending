How It Works

From application to funded in days.

Our streamlined process connects borrowers with lenders quickly, fairly, and transparently.

01
Apply & Get Scored
Borrowers submit their loan purpose, credit history, and financials. Our system assigns a risk tier within 24 hours.
02
Loan Listed
Approved loans appear on our marketplace with full transparency — purpose, term, risk badge, and EMI breakdown.
03
Lenders Fund
Individual lenders browse listings, use the calculator to model returns, and commit capital in ₹5,000 increments.
04
Repay & Earn
Monthly EMIs are auto-debited from borrowers. Lenders receive principal + interest directly to their dashboard.
